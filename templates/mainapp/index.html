Our School AT A GLANCE

Established in 2018, Daarus-salaam model college, DSMC as it is fondly referred to, is the one of the best colleges in Nigeria. Until 2020 when it became a full-fledged independent college, it was a College of the University of London in a special relationship scheme. The college, which took off with academic programmes in Arts, Science, Engineering and Commercial, is now a comprehensive citadel of learning with academic programmes in sixteen Faculties namely, Arts, Science, Basic Medical Sciences, Clinical Sciences, Agriculture, the Social Sciences, Education, Veterinary Medicine, Pharmacy, Technology, Law, Public Health, Dentistry, Economics, Renewable Natural Resources and Environmental Design and Management.
